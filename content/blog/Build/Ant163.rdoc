= Ant 1.6.3 Available

abstract:: 
Ant 1.6.3 is now available for your automation pleasure.

{Ant 1.6.3}[http://ant.apache.org/] is now available for your
automation pleasure.  This release includes a {beefy
list}[http://www.apache.org/dist/ant/RELEASE-NOTES-1.6.3.html] of
fixed bugs and enhancements, some of which are related to the new
{subant}[http://www.pragmaticautomation.com/cgi-bin/pragauto.cgi/Build/Ant16Subant.rdoc],
{macrodef}[http://www.pragmaticautomation.com/cgi-bin/pragauto.cgi/Build/Ant16Macrodef.rdoc],
and
{import}[http://www.pragmaticautomation.com/cgi-bin/pragauto.cgi/Build/Ant16Import.rdoc]
tasks we recently toured.
